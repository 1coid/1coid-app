Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your account, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the Profile tab within the app.
  2. Click on menu option on top left.
  3. Locate the "Delete Account" option.
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to submit your account deletion request.

You can also request account deletion by contacting our support team at support@1coid.com.
